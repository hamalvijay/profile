About us

Supporting You, Every Step of the Way

At Helping Hands, we are proud to be a registered NDIS provider committed to empowering individuals with disabilities to live their fullest, most independent lives. Based in Australia, our team brings compassion, professionalism, and genuine care to every participant we support.

Who We Are

We are a dedicated group of support workers, coordinators, and professionals with years of experience in disability care and community support. Our mission is to provide tailored, person-centred services that put you first. Whether you need help with daily activities, community participation, or navigating your NDIS plan — we’re here to walk alongside you.

What We Stand For

  • Respect: Every individual has the right to dignity, choice, and independence.
  • Trust: We believe in transparent communication and dependable care.
  • Empowerment: Our goal is to give you control over your care and decisions.
  • Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and provide culturally sensitive services for all.
