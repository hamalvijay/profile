About us

Supporting You, Every Step of the Way

At Bloomence Care Services, we are proud to be a registered NDIS provider committed to empowering individuals with disabilities to live their fullest, most independent lives. Based in Australia, our team brings compassion, professionalism, and genuine care to every participant we support.

Who We Are

We are a dedicated group of support workers, coordinators, and professionals with years of experience in disability care and community support. Our mission is to provide tailored, person-centred services that put you first. Whether you need help with daily activities, community participation, or navigating your NDIS plan — we’re here to walk alongside you.

What We Stand For

  • Respect: Every individual has the right to dignity, choice, and independence.
  • Trust: We believe in transparent communication and dependable care.
  • Empowerment: Our goal is to give you control over your care and decisions.
  • Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and provide culturally sensitive services for all.

Every morning, Sarah greets James with a smile as she helps him get ready for the day. James, a spirited young man who uses a wheelchair, loves attending art workshops and visiting the local community center. With the support of our dedicated NDIS worker Sarah, he’s not just participating — he’s thriving. At Helping Hands, we believe in more than just assistance. We believe in empowering people like James to live life with confidence, purpose, and joy.

Amira’s smile shines as brightly as the afternoon sun while she enjoys a peaceful walk through the local park. Surrounded by blooming flowers and birdsong, she chats happily with her support worker who walks beside her. Living with a physical disability once limited her time outdoors — but with the care and encouragement from our Helping Hands team, Amira now feels confident, independent, and connected to the world around her. These are the moments that remind us why we do what we do.
